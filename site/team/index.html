Team / About Us

Meet our team members and mentors.

Team Members

  • Freddy Rivera – Project Manager
    Oversees overall project coordination, scheduling, communication, and documentation. Ensures cross-team alignment between mechanical and electrical subsystems, manages prototype integration, and leads client presentations and deliverables.
  • Andres Gonzales – System Controls Lead
    Responsible for designing and tuning the control algorithms for both robots, including PID development, sensor integration, and real-time software behavior. Collaborates closely with the electrical team to ensure stable and accurate system response.
  • Colin Parsinia – Manufacturing Lead (Robot 1)
    Leads CAD modeling and fabrication for Robot 1, including 3D-printed components, mechanical assemblies, and fit-and-function testing. Ensures manufacturability, structural integrity, and compatibility with the electrical subsystem.
  • Florence Fasugbe – Manufacturing Lead (Robot 2)
    Responsible for CAD development and prototyping of Robot 2’s mechanical components, including the dynamic beam mechanism and platform supports. Supports iterative testing and refinement based on system performance and client feedback.
  • Ziyi Tang – Budget Liaison
    Manages project purchasing, tracks spending, and ensures the team stays within budget constraints. Coordinates part sourcing, lead times, and vendor communication to maintain smooth prototyping progress throughout the semester.
